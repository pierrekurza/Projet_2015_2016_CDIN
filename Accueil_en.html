Collaborative Tools


The notion of collaborative work refers to a way of working that isn't based off traditional organisation, it's more specificly a new way of working (eventually integrated in a production economy model) where many people collaborate thanks to information and communication technologies, especially internet platforms.
The new IT tools allow to maximize creativity and efficiency of an associated group in major projects even if they are dispersed in space and time.
