University Institute of Technology (IUT) of Lens

In short...

The University Institute of Technology of Lens was created in 1986. It was attached to the University of Artois in 1993. It's near downtown, the Bollaert stadium and the Louvre-Lens on 4.5 hectares.

Each year, the IUT welcomes 1 200 students in 5 courses (but only proposes 4) :

  • Commerce
  • Management
  • IT
  • Digital Medias
  • Service for health
