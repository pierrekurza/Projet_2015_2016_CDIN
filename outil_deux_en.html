Videoconferences

It allows you to interact with people by putting in relation Audio and Video.
It's very useful for long distance intercations.
One of the most known and used ones would be Skype.

What's Required

Videoconferences requires:

  • A Videoconferences software
  • A Microphone and speakers for the audio
  • A Webcam for the video
  • An Internet connexion

What Videoconferences to choose ?

There is multiple types of Videoconferences.
Most of the time we talk about Videoconferences on softwares.
But recently Videoconferences directly on websites has developped really fast.

Advantages & Flaws

Advantages

There is many advantages to Videoconferences:

  • Online meetings are possible all over the world.
  • No need to move.
  • Realtime reactions.
  • Sharing documents is possible.
  • Free according to each software

Flaws

On the other hand, there is some flaws:

  • High-Quality can be expensive.
  • Lack of security on some softwares.
  • No Human/Physical contact.
  • Technical problems can happen.
  • Jet lag if the participants are spared across the world.

Most popular softwares

Here's the most used Videoconferences softwares:

  1. Skype

Exemples

First of all, here's Skype, probably the most used of all Videoconferences softwares.
It's very simple to use, and in addition to Videoconferences, it allows to send messages and files..





Skype's principal competitor is ooVoo which particularity is that it allows yu to leave a vocal message if the person doesn't responds a call.
You can also synchronise your contacts with you e-mail adress...





Finally: iVisit, a very good softwares that allows to show files (Word,PDF..), so all the participants can see simultaneously.
Loclaisation is also possible with iVisit thanks to a GPS integrated in the software.
